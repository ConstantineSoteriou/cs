Who I Am

My name is Constantinos Soteriou. I am 32 years old and I am a web & graphic designer & web developer based in Cyprus, with over 5 years of freelance and studio experience. Computers have been in my life since I was a child and I always loved anything related with pixels so I started to design at a young age. Most of my skills and knowledge are self taught. I have a passion for designing detailed, creative and modern websites & graphics. I like to keep an eye on the latest trends over typography, shapes, colours, etc. I have been working as a web-designer (freelance and studio) for over 4 years, and have done lots of work over that time. My skills cover many aspects of graphic design, web design, illustration and technical knowledge. I am rather skilled on hardware, software, and computer knowledge too.
